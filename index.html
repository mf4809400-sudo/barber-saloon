Our House

Where every detail is measured, every cut deliberate.

Tucked inside the Pakland Business Center at I-8 Markaz, Razors & Scissors is more than a barbershop — it's a quiet retreat for men who appreciate craftsmanship. From the warm leather of our chairs to the soft glow of our backlit mirrors, every detail is designed to slow you down.
